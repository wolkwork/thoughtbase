## Overview

Team management allows you to collaborate with others on managing feedback, ideas, and changelogs.

## Adding Team Members

### Inviting Members

1. Go to **Members** in the sidebar
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the member's email address
4. Choose their role:
   - **Admin**: Full access to all features
   - **Member**: Can manage ideas and changelogs
5. Send the invitation

### Member Roles

- **Admin**: 
  - Full access to all features
  - Can manage settings
  - Can invite and remove members
  - Can manage billing
  
- **Member**:
  - Can manage ideas and changelogs
  - Can view and edit content
  - Cannot access settings or billing

## Managing Members

### Viewing Members

The Members page shows:
- All team members and their roles
- When they joined
- Last active date
- Current status

### Removing Members

- Remove members who no longer need access
- This action cannot be undone
- The member will lose access immediately

### Resending Invitations

- Resend invitations if they expire
- Check invitation status in the members list

## Best Practices

### Role Management

- **Principle of least privilege**: Give members only the access they need
- **Regular reviews**: Periodically review team members and roles
- **Clear responsibilities**: Define who should have admin access

### Collaboration

- **Use comments**: Collaborate on ideas through comments
- **Assign ownership**: Use ideas to track who's working on what
- **Regular communication**: Keep the team aligned on priorities

### Security

- **Remove inactive members**: Clean up access for former team members
- **Review permissions**: Regularly audit who has access
- **Secure invitations**: Only invite trusted team members

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Start small**: Begin with a few admins, add members as needed
2. **Document roles**: Keep track of who has what access
3. **Regular cleanup**: Remove members who are no longer active
4. **Use notifications**: Keep team members informed of important updates
