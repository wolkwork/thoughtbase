## Creating an Account

1. Sign up for a Thoughtbase account
2. Create your first organization (workspace)
3. You'll be automatically added as an admin member

## Accessing Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard where you can:
- View all your organizations (workspaces)
- Switch between organizations
- Access organization-specific features

## Navigation

The main navigation sidebar includes:

- **Dashboard**: Overview of your organization
- **Ideas**: Manage feedback and ideas
- **Changelog**: Create and publish product updates
- **Roadmap**: Visual view of what's planned and in progress
- **Members**: Manage your team
- **Settings**: Configure your organization
