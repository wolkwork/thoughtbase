## Overview

The **Roadmap** page gives you a visual overview of what's planned and in progress. It helps you prioritize work and communicate plans to your team and users.

## Roadmap Columns

The roadmap is organized into columns:

- **Reviewing**: Newly submitted ideas awaiting review. These won't show up publicly until approved.
- **Open**: Ideas that are open for voting and discussion.
- **Planned**: Ideas scheduled for future work
- **In Progress**: Ideas currently being worked on
- **Completed**: Ideas that have been finished and released
- **Archived**: Ideas that are either completed or not pursued. Archived ideas are hidden from the public view.
