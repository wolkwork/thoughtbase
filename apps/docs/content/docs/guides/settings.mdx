## Accessing Settings

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar to configure your organization. Settings are organized into tabs:

- **Billing**: Manage your subscription
- **SSO**: Configure Single Sign-On
- **Branding**: Customize appearance
- **Team**: Manage team settings

## Billing

Manage your subscription and payment:

- **View current plan**: See what plan you're on
- **Update payment methods**: Add or change payment information
- **View billing history**: See past invoices and payments
- **Upgrade or downgrade**: Change your plan as needed

## SSO (Single Sign-On)

Configure SSO to allow users from your external application to authenticate with the widget.

### Setting Up SSO

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **SSO** tab
2. Generate or view your SSO secret
3. Use this secret to sign JWTs in your application
4. Users can then authenticate using the widget with SSO tokens

### SSO Secret

- **Generate**: Create a new secret if you don't have one
- **Rotate**: Regularly rotate your secret for security
- **Keep secure**: Never expose your secret in client-side code

**Important**: Keep your SSO secret secure! Rotate it regularly for security.

For detailed SSO integration instructions, see the [Widget SSO Integration Guide](/widget/guides/sso-integration).

## Branding

Customize your organization's appearance:

### Organization Name

- Set your organization's display name
- This appears in the dashboard and public pages

### Logo

- Upload your organization logo
- Recommended size: Square format, at least 256x256 pixels
- Maximum file size: 4.5MB
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG

### Colors

- Customize primary colors (if available)
- Colors affect public pages and widget appearance

## Team Settings

Manage team-wide settings:

- **View team activity**: See what your team has been doing
- **Configure permissions**: Set default permissions for new members
- **Team preferences**: Configure organization-wide preferences

## Best Practices

### Security

- **Rotate SSO secrets**: Change secrets regularly
- **Review access**: Regularly audit who has access
- **Secure credentials**: Never share secrets or credentials

### Branding

- **Consistent branding**: Use your logo and colors consistently
- **Professional appearance**: Keep branding professional and on-brand
- **Regular updates**: Update branding as your brand evolves

### Billing

- **Monitor usage**: Keep an eye on your plan limits
- **Update payment methods**: Keep payment information current
- **Plan appropriately**: Choose a plan that fits your needs

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Test SSO**: Test SSO integration in a development environment first
2. **Backup secrets**: Store SSO secrets securely (password manager)
3. **Brand consistency**: Use the same logo across all platforms
4. **Regular reviews**: Periodically review all settings
