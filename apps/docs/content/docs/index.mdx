## What is Thoughtbase?

Thoughtbase is a feedback management platform that helps product teams:

- **Collect Feedback**: Gather ideas and suggestions from users through an embeddable widget
- **Prioritize Roadmap**: Organize and track what's in progress and planned
- **Share Updates**: Publish changelogs to keep users informed about product improvements
