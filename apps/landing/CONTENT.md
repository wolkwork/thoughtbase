## The Feedback OS

ThoughtBase isn't just "customer support software." It's a suite of tools built to help you ship the right things, faster.

### 1. Feedback Boards (Issues for Users)

Your users have ideas. Give them a place to dump them so they don't clog up your Discord or email.

- **Voting & Emojis:** Let users fight over what matters (so you don't have to guess).
- **Semantic Deduplication:** `Ctrl+F` for feature requests, automatically.
- **Internal & Private Boards:** For internal dogfooding or VIP customers.

### 2. Roadmap (Auto-sync)

Stop manually updating a Notion page that nobody looks at.

- **Status Sync:** Move a ticket to "Done", roadmap updates instantly.
- **Vote Visibility:** Show stakeholders _why_ you're building X instead of Y.

### 3. Changelog (Release Notes as Code)

Writing changelogs sucks. Let AI do it.

- **Auto-drafting:** We parse your shipped features and write the update.
- **Notifications:** We email everyone who voted for the thing you just shipped.
- **In-app Widget:** Users see the "New" badge and actually read what changed.
